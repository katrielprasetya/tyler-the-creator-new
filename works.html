OUT NOW.

On April 13, 2024, Okonma performed a headliner set at Coachella, closing out Saturday night with a performance that included guest appearances from Childish Gambino, ASAP Rocky, and Kali Uchis along with a duet of "Earfquake" with Charlie Wilson. He began the set bursting through the wall of a fake trailer using pyrotechnics, before performing tracks from across his discography. Critics labeled the performance as an "exhilarating, high-stakes spectacle", and a "brilliant reminder of a cult hero's power". On October 16, 2024, Okonma revealed the name of his next album as Chromakopia in a teaser video titled "St. Chroma". On October 21, Okonma released the single, "Noid". The album was released on October 28.

Works.

In 2017, Okonma released Flower Boy, which earned him widespread critical acclaim and commercial success. Subsequent studio albums Igor (2019) and Call Me If You Get Lost (2021) debuted at number one on the Billboard 200 and won Best Rap Album at the 2020 and 2022 Grammy Awards, respectively. His eighth studio album, Chromakopia (2024), debuted at number one with the highest first-week sales of his career.
